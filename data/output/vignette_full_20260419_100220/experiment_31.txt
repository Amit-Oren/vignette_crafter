This cycle is profoundly isolating. Her marriage is strained by her irritability and withdrawal, and

threat, fueled by her intrusive memories and reinforced by her belief in her own helplessness,
maintains her PTSD in a self-perpetuating loop, leaving her feeling trapped and disconnected from
